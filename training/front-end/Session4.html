Session 4

Please follow the steps listed below:

  • Press F12 key
  • Click 'Console' or press ESC key
  • To get the first capicua numbers in a range just type: getCapicua(range lower value, range higher value):
    e.g. getCapicua(10,50)
    The result will be the first capicua number in that range, in this case 11
  • To know the first N odd/even numbers type printOddEven(N)
    e.g. printOddEven(4)
    this will return the first 4 odd/even numbers, in this case: 2,4,6,8 and 1,3,5,7
  • To get the factorial of a number just type: fact(n):
    e.g. fact(4)
    The result will be the factorial of 4, in this case 24
